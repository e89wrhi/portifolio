**Overview:**  
Horizon Shop is an **e-commerce platform backend** created for medium-sized online businesses.  
It follows a **modular monolith approach** with **domain-driven design**, ensuring scalability while keeping deployment simple.  

**Key Features:**  
- REST and GraphQL APIs  
- Authentication with JWT + OAuth2  
- Integrated payment modules (Stripe, PayPal)  
- Event-driven order processing system  
- Monitoring, caching, and CI/CD pipelines  

**Status:**  
🚧 In Development – Public beta planned Q1 2025
