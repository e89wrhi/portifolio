**Overview:**  
Solstice Agency’s website combines **Figma-first design** with **Next.js 13 App Router**.  
It highlights services, case studies, and a modern blog section with animations.  

**Key Features:**  
- Custom hero animations with Framer Motion  
- CMS-powered blog (Sanity.io)  
- TailwindCSS for rapid styling  
- Optimized for SEO & performance

✅ Live – [View Solstice Agency](https://solstice.agency)
