**Overview:**  
Pulse is a **React Native Expo** app for fitness enthusiasts to track workouts, monitor nutrition, and stay motivated.  

**Key Features:**  
- Daily workout logging  
- Meal & calorie tracker  
- Push notifications for reminders  
- Sync with Apple Health & Google Fit  
- Offline-first with SQLite  

**Status:**  
🚀 Beta Testing – Available on TestFlight & Google Play Beta
